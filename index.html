About Me


I am a highly passionate and dedicated software developer with extensive experience in creating scalable, efficient, and high-performance applications. My expertise spans a wide array of technologies,
but I am particularly specialized in designing advanced graphical user interfaces (GUIs) and developing reliable embedded systems.
With a solid educational background in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from IIS Ettore Majorana,
I have developed a deep understanding of both hardware and software integration. This unique combination of theoretical knowledge and hands-on experience allows me to design intuitive and responsive interfaces while solving complex technical challenges in embedded systems development.
Over the course of my career, I have honed my skills in several key areas, including user interface design, algorithm development, and communication protocol implementation. My programming proficiency spans a variety of languages, such as C, Python, and C#, which I leverage to deliver robust, efficient, and maintainable code.
In addition to my technical expertise in software development, I have significant experience in firmware development. This includes writing low-level code to interact directly with hardware components, as well as optimizing firmware for resource-constrained embedded systems. I am proficient in designing firmware for a variety of embedded platforms, from microcontrollers to complex real-time systems. My work in firmware development involves not only ensuring the functionality

I have a proven track record in implementing and optimizing communication protocols, such as Serial, TCP/IP, ModBus, and CANbus. These protocols are critical to ensuring the functionality and reliability of embedded solutions across multiple industries, including automotive, energy, and structural monitoring. By combining my technical abilities with a keen eye for detail and a problem-solving mindset, I have contributed to numerous successful projects that enhance system
performance, optimize resource usage, and increase overall reliability.

I have mastered HTML for creating structured and semantic web pages, CSS for styling and laying out attractive, user-friendly interfaces, and JavaScript for adding interactivity and dynamic functionality. My ability to learn and apply
these skills independently has allowed me to create websites that are not only visually appealing but also performant and accessible across devices and screen sizes.
As I continue to grow in my career, I am excited to take on new challenges that allow me to apply my skills to innovative projects and contribute to the development of cutting-edge technology solutions, both in software, firmware, and web development.
